Ice Fruit in Blox Fruits

All about the Ice Fruit in Blox Fruits:

General Facts

  • Type: Elemental
  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Price: 350,000 Beli or 750 Robux
  • Awakening Cost: 14,500 Fragments
  • Best For: Stunning enemies, bounty hunting, and PvP

Abilities (Unawakened)

  • Ice Spears (Z): Fires multiple ice shards at enemies.
  • Ice Surge (X): Creates a wave of ice that knocks back enemies.
  • Ice Bird (C): Launches an ice bird that freezes enemies on impact.
  • Glacial Epoch (V): Covers the ground in ice, freezing enemies.
  • Frost Walker (F): Allows the user to walk on water by freezing it.

Awakened Abilities

  • Cold Storm (Z): Launches a tornado-like ice projectile that stuns enemies.
  • Glacial Surge (X): Releases a powerful ice burst with knockback.
  • Frozen Dragon (C): Summons an ice dragon that crashes down.
  • Absolute Zero (V): Creates a massive ice field that freezes enemies.
  • Frozen Path (F): Upgraded Frost Walker with faster movement.

Why Ice Fruit is Popular

  • Strong Stuns: Great for PvP and bounty hunting.
  • Water Walking: Allows easy travel across the sea.
  • Combo Potential: Pairs well with swords and fighting styles.
  • PvP Viability: Awakened Ice Fruit has powerful crowd control.

Trivia

  • Ice Fruit was one of the first fruits added to Blox Fruits.
  • It grants immunity to water hazards by freezing the surface.
  • The titles "The Ice Queen" and "The Ice King" are unlocked upon awakening.
